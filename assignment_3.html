intent

for my last project, i decided to use the countdown between winter solstice and summer solstice by creating interactive daily cards to help users get through and appreciate the winter/spring months. i added written components to convey a personal touch: asking individual questions regarding their current state of mind, intentions and creating activities to keep them entertained and busy during the days leading up to the summer solstice

process

i started creating individual cards on illustrator with written elements that involved advice, questions and prompts relating to getting through and finding enjoyment in the days leading up to the summer solstice. i utilized the element of light by contrasting the shades, graph and type on each card. i wanted to factor in the countdown within the cards without being too literal (numbered or signed). i finally decided on creating a sun graph to showcase the gradual growth and amount of sunlight we get between december and june. i then created a grid structure that made the increase of the graph more manageable to design. i started to rearrange the type to sit at the bottom of the canvas instead of the centre to keep the graph the main focus while still having the written element legible.
